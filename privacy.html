Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24 2025

DocSnap does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All scanning, processing, and PDF generation is performed locally on your device. No internet connection is required or used for document processing.

We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party SDKs of any kind.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/jamesbabiak/SimpleDocScanner
